About Potato Disease Detection

Potato diseases are a major threat to farmers worldwide, especially in developing countries like Ethiopia, where potatoes are a key staple crop. These diseases, caused by various bacteria, fungi, and viruses, can drastically reduce yields and crop quality.

In Ethiopia, potato farmers face challenges from diseases such as late blight, bacterial wilt, and early blight. Early detection and treatment of these diseases are crucial to prevent large-scale crop loss.

Our Potato Disease Detection system utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze images of potato plants, identify signs of diseases, and provide recommendations to improve crop health.

The system aims to empower farmers with real-time, actionable insights, improving crop yields and ensuring food security.
